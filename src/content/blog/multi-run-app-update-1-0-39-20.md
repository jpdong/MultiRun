We are excited to announce the release of Multi-Run App version 1.0.39.20! This update brings a highly requested feature—a subscription service—along with significant performance improvements to enhance your experience.

### Subscription Service

Many of you have asked for a way to remove ads, and we've listened. With our new subscription service, you can enjoy an ad-free experience and unlock exclusive features.

**Subscription Benefits:**

*   **Ad-Free:** Enjoy Multi-Run without any interruptions.
*   **Secret Zone:** Gain access to our exclusive Secret Zone, with more surprises to come.
*   **Modify Space Info:** Customize your spaces with the ability to modify space information.
*   **Faster Speed:** Experience quicker installation and launch times for all your apps.

For first-time subscribers, we are offering a **1-month free trial**. After the trial, the subscription is just **$4.99 per month**.

### Crash Fixes and Performance Optimization

In this update, we have also focused on making the app more stable and responsive. We have addressed several crashing issues and optimized the app to reduce lag, ensuring a smoother and more reliable experience.

We are committed to providing you with the best multi-account experience. Thank you for your continued support and feedback. Update to version 1.0.39.20 now and let us know what you think!
