### 1. Change Icon: Total Customization at Your Fingertips

Let's start with one of the most persistent and frustrating challenges of running multiple instances of the same application: visual clutter. When you clone an application, the default behavior of the operating system is to simply duplicate the original app's icon. This immediately creates a massive point of friction on your home screen. How many times have you stared at your phone, looking at two identical WhatsApp, Facebook, or mobile game icons, and accidentally tapped the wrong one? Sending a casual personal message on your professional account or logging into the wrong gaming server is an annoyance we wanted to eliminate entirely. 

Enter the brand-new "Change Icon" feature—a cornerstone of the 1.0.43.20 update! We strongly believe that your device should uniquely reflect your personal workflow, and this powerful personalization tool allows you to completely customize the visual identity of your dual-opened or cloned applications. You are no longer bound by identical icons and confusingly similar app labels.

For this initial launch phase, we have integrated a carefully curated, beautifully designed selection of tool-themed emojis that serve as your new custom icon resource library. The implementation is incredibly straightforward and user-friendly: when creating or editing a cloned app, simply open the new customization menu and browse through our emoji selection. Want to assign a sleek briefcase emoji to your corporate messaging app clone to immediately distinguish it from your personal chats? You can easily do that. Prefer a game controller or a rocket to easily spot your alternative gaming account? It's just a tap away. You can even use a lock or shield emoji to visually designate a private, secure instance of a browser. 

This feature does significantly more than just make your home screen look uniquely yours; it fundamentally improves everyday usability. By assigning distinct visual cues to your clones, muscle memory takes over faster, making navigating your app drawer an absolute breeze. While we are starting with this focused set of tool emojis, we are fully committed to expanding this feature in upcoming patches, giving you even more creative freedom and an even wider array of icons to choose from. 

### 2. Embracing the Shadows: A Beautiful, System-Wide Dark Mode

If there is one absolute truth in modern software development, it is this: users absolutely love Dark Mode. For the past year, "Please add Dark Mode" has been the single most frequent request across all of our communication channels—dominating our community forums, filling our customer support inbox, and standing out prominently in our app store reviews. We heard you loud and clear, and we wanted to make sure we got the implementation absolutely perfect before releasing it to the public. 

We are incredibly proud to announce that with the 1.0.43.20 update, the Multi Run app is officially embracing the shadows with a comprehensive, system-wide Dark Mode! We didn't want to take the easy way out and simply invert the existing color palette, as that often leads to washed-out text, harsh contrast ratios, and an overall visually unappealing experience. Instead, our UI/UX design team painstakingly went back to the drawing board to entirely rebuild our color system from the ground up. Every single screen, popup menu, settings panel, and toggle switch has been individually optimized for a true, deep, and brilliant dark aesthetic.

The tangible benefits of our new Dark Mode extend far beyond simply looking incredibly sleek and modern. In our modern, screen-heavy world, bright white interfaces can become agonizingly painful, particularly when you are managing your apps late at night or in dimly lit environments. Dark Mode dramatically reduces blue light emission and screen glare, alleviating eye strain and preventing visual fatigue. Furthermore, if you are using a smartphone or tablet equipped with an OLED or AMOLED display, our true-black backgrounds physically turn off screen pixels, which can lead to highly measurable improvements in your overall battery life.

The integration into your daily life is completely seamless. By default, Multi Run's appearance will automatically mirror your device's global system settings. However, we also strongly believe in user agency, so we have included an overriding manual toggle right within our settings tab. Whether you prefer the crisp, clean look of our original light theme or the sleek, immersive comfort of the new dark aesthetic, the power is entirely in your hands.
